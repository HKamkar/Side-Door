# Question banks

Source pool for generating tailored interview questions. Pick from the relevant bank based on role and stage, then customize each question to the specific JD and the user's resume.

## Behavioral — universal

- Tell me about yourself.
- Walk me through your resume.
- Why are you leaving your current role / why now?
- Why this company specifically?
- Why this role?
- Tell me about a time you failed. What did you learn?
- Tell me about a time you disagreed with a manager / peer / decision. How did you handle it?
- Tell me about a time you had to deliver under a tight deadline.
- Tell me about your proudest project. What made it hard?
- Tell me about a time you got feedback that surprised you.
- What's a weakness you're working on?
- Where do you want to be in 3–5 years?
- What's your dream role?

## Behavioral — leadership / scope

- Tell me about a time you led a team through a major change.
- Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information.
- How do you handle a low performer on your team?
- Tell me about a time you mentored someone. What happened to them?
- How do you decide what NOT to do?
- Tell me about a hire that worked out, and one that didn't.

## Technical — software engineering IC

- Design a system that does X. (Use the JD to pick X.)
- Walk me through a bug you debugged that took longer than it should have.
- What's a technical decision you regret?
- How would you optimize this query / function / API?
- Tell me about the most complex thing you've shipped.
- What's your testing philosophy?
- How do you handle code review when you disagree with the reviewer?
- Coding: typical LeetCode-style or pair-programming on a real-world snippet.

## Technical — product manager

- Walk me through a feature you shipped. Why that one, not something else?
- How do you prioritize when stakeholders disagree?
- Tell me about a metric you defined. Why that metric?
- A user complains X is broken. How do you investigate?
- Design a new feature for our product. (Or theirs.)
- What do you do when engineering says it'll take 3x longer than you scoped?

## Technical — data / ML

- Walk me through an analysis you did. What was the question?
- How do you choose between two models when one is more accurate and one is more interpretable?
- Tell me about a data quality issue you caught — what tipped you off?
- A stakeholder asks you to prove X. The data is ambiguous. What do you do?

## Curveballs

- What questions do you have for us?
- What's your salary expectation?
- What other companies are you interviewing with?
- Why did you leave [company X]?
- Why was the gap in your resume?
- We can't pay what you're asking — would you take less?
- Why should we hire you over the other candidates?

## How to use this bank

For each interview, pick:
- 4–6 universal behavioral, customized so the question naturally pulls a resume bullet the user actually has a story for.
- 4–6 technical from the relevant role bank, customized to the must-haves in the JD.
- 2–4 curveballs the JD or company context makes likely.

For each picked question, write:
- The question (as the interviewer would ask it).
- 3–5 bullet skeleton of an answer using the user's real experience.
- What NOT to say (1 line — common trap).
- Stories from the user's resume the answer should lift.
